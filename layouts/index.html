Why AGTI

AGTI delivers a full stack AI solution to institutional investors integrating AI into their process

Verified Inference

AGTI chatbots as well as API integrations allow you to query AI systems completely privately with blockchain based verification that you are receiving an exact model with precise settings. This allows for scalable but private backtesting without hosting your own hardware while also being able to prove / record the specific AI application for trade generation.

Synthetic Portfolio Managers

AGTI has a framework of Synthetic Portfolio Managers (or SPMs). Similar to Anthropic's MCP, but designed for trading - the SPM framework allows you to integrate data sets, backtests, prompts, and tool use in a standardized way. This allows you to track AI based traders and manage their risk similar to a multi manager fund.
